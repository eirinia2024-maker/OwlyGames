How to play

1) Flip two cards.
2) If they match, answer the is/are/am quiz.
3) Correct → +1 point and you keep your turn.
4) Wrong → 0 points and the turn switches.
5) Match all pairs to win!

Keyboard: Use Tab + Enter on cards. Escape closes modals.
